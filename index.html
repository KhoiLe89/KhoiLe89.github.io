Locus

A travel app for the "non-traveler"

There are so many travel apps out there these days to help you rent-a-car, book hotels, find restaurants, so on and so on; but what if I dont know where to go? Well Locus is designed around the non-experienced traveler. An app that allows the less experienced traveler to review the personal and intimate stories/pictures of those who have more experience seeing the world to gain insight on what the world has to offer. RentMap

Rent Price Application

RentMap helps users make informed decisions on where to live. Using GoogleMaps API, this web-app makes it easy for viewers to see the average rent price of a Zip-Code; along with nearby restaurants, schools, gyms, and grocery stores.
